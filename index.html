Smart University in the IoT

Bachelor Project @ iNET RG, HAW Hamburg

About

SmartUniversity is a seminar course for bachelor students of computer science and electrical engineering at the Hamburg University of Applied Sciences HAW. It is scheduled for the winter semester 2015/16 and is organized by the iNET research group.

In the following you find further information on the designated schedule and the student projects themselves. Even more details can be found on GitHub: @smartuni.
